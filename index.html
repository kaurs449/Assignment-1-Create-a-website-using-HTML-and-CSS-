DR. MANMOHAN SINGH

13th Prime Minister of India

Manmohan Singh (born 26 September 1932) is an Indian politician, economist, academician and bureaucrat who served as the 13th Prime Minister of India from 2004 to 2014. He is the third longest-serving prime minister after Jawaharlal Nehru and Indira Gandhi. A member of the Indian National Congress, Singh was the first Sikh and non-Hindu prime minister of India.

The following list is a time line of Dr. Manmohan Singh Life

  • 1970 He was named to a series of economic advisory posts with the Indian government and became a frequent consultant to prime ministers.
  • 1976–80 Dr. Singh also worked at the Reserve Bank of India, serving as director
  • 1982–85 Manmohan Singh also worked at the Reserve Bank of India, serving as governor
  • 1991 He was named as finance minister
  • 1991 For Congress, Dr. Singh ran for the Lok Sabha
  • 2004 In parliamentary elections, he defeated the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)
